FL-SLD DRAWING

What is FL-SLD?
FL-SLD is an invented technique to draw MV networks:
1- provides a bird-eye view of the full network in one drawing:
2-lets you easily figure out how complex or meshed the network is
3-lets you easily figure out the status of source diversification
4- Provides an easily searchable and editable drawing
Eventualy lets you confidently take the next step to replan your network to meet standards
