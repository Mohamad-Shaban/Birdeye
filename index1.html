FL-SLD DRAWING

What is FL-SLD?
FL-SLD is an invented technique to draw MV networks:
1- Provides a bird's-eye view of the full network in one drawing.
2- Lets you easily figure out how complex or meshed the network is.
3- Helps in assessing source diversification status.
4- Provides an easily searchable and editable drawing.
Ultimately, it allows you to confidently take the next step in replanning your network to meet standards.
